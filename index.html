Bomber Tilt is a blockchain-based strategy game where players can collect,
trade and use 'bomberts' NFT (non-fungible tokens) to play and collect bombs scattered throughout
the scene in order to obtain rewards. As the game is based on blockchain, players can be sure that their
'Bomberts' NFTs are truly unique and have real value. How to play: Players can start with only one Bombert or
up to a maximum of 10, available in the official game 'shop', in special events or from other players in the
sales market.It is possible to play with a free Bombert, but this Bombert is not an NFT item, however,
with it it is possible to obtain the game's alternative currency called Bchips, Bchips do not have real value
on the blockchain, but with them it is possible to trade for TILT (TLT) tokens and subsequently with TLT obtain
Bomberts NFT.Technologies related to web3 will provide great achievements in the field of games, competitiveness,
exclusive markets, inclusion, diversity and opportunities. We are preparing for this great revolution and willing
to evolve this entire ecosystem.
